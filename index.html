CAMEL

Some Interesting Facts of Camel
  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
  3. Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!

    PIGEON

    Some Interesting Facts of Pigeon
    1. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.
    2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities. They use a range of skills, such as using the sun as a guide and an internal ‘magnetic compass
    3. Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young
